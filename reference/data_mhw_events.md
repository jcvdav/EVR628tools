# Marine Heatwaves in TURF-managed fisheries

Marine heatwave events detected for one location

## Usage

``` r
data_mhw_events
```

## Format

\## \`data_mhw_events\` A data.frame with 82 rows and 5 columns:

- date_peak:

  Date - The date in which the event was at its maximum

- intensity_mean:

  Numeric - Mean MHW intensity, in °C

- intensity_max:

  Numeric - Max MHW intensity, in °C

- intensity_cumulative:

  Numeric - Cumulative MHW intensity, in °C days

## Source

Villaseñor-Derbez, J.C., Arafeh-Dalmau, N. & Micheli, F. Past and future
impacts of marine heatwaves on small-scale fisheries in Baja California,
Mexico. Commun Earth Environ 5, 623 (2024).
<https://doi.org/10.1038/s43247-024-01696-x>
