# Marine Heatwaves in TURF-managed fisheries

Daily time series of sea surface temperature, seasonality, and marine
heatwave thresholds for a single site

## Usage

``` r
data_mhw_ts
```

## Format

\## \`data_mhw_ts\` A data.frame with 1431 rows and 4 columns:

- date:

  Date - The date

- temp:

  Numeric - Sea surface temperature, in °C

- seas:

  Numeric - Seasonality, in °C

- thresh:

  Numeric - Threshold to detect as marine heatwave, in °C

## Source

Villaseñor-Derbez, J.C., Arafeh-Dalmau, N. & Micheli, F. Past and future
impacts of marine heatwaves on small-scale fisheries in Baja California,
Mexico. Commun Earth Environ 5, 623 (2024).
<https://doi.org/10.1038/s43247-024-01696-x>
